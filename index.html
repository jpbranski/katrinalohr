My Story, My Mission

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Katrina Lohr, and I am a dedicated and passionate interior designer currently pursuing an Associate Degree in Interior Design and Kitchen & Bath Design at Waukesha County Technical College.

With a solid foundation in design principles, honed through my academic journey and hands-on experiences, I have developed a keen eye for aesthetics and functionality. My portfolio showcases a diverse range of projects, reflecting my creativity, attention to detail, and commitment to delivering exceptional design solutions. Each piece highlights my ability to transform spaces into harmonious and inspiring environments that meet the unique needs and preferences of clients.

I invite you to explore my work and see how I bring vision to life through thoughtful design.
